About

I'm Lyana, a 16-year-old artist passionate about creativity, self- expression, and making a positive impact. I've been drawing and painting since childhood, and my art has been featured in local galleries and exhibitions. I love experimenting with colors, techniques, and mediums to create unique pieces. In addition to art, I enjoy reading, writing, and engaging in thoughtful conversations. I believe language has the power to connect us across cultures and borders, and I'm always eager to learn more about diverse perspectives and experiences. My goal is to use my art to make a difference in the world, bringing people together and inspiring positive change."
